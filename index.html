How it works

Nothing depends on our word

Four steps, all verifiable by anyone on the Solana blockchain. There's no discretionary distribution or promises: the contract does exactly what it says, and not even the team can change it after deployment.

01

Initial buy on pump.fun

The creator buys a percentage of the supply at launch, just like any other buyer — no free allocation, no hidden presale.

02

Locked in a public contract

The portion earmarked for burning is deposited into a scheduled-release contract (Streamflow), marked as non-cancelable and non-revocable from minute one.

03

Automatic daily release

Every day, with no human intervention, the contract releases 2,000,000 tokens directly to a recognized burn address. There's no button to press, no decision to make.

04

Supply reduction, forever

Those tokens leave circulation permanently. Over 126 days, every burn is public, with its transaction hash visible the moment it happens.
